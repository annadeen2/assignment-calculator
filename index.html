The CDC recently announced that anyone who is fully vaccinated can travel safely within the United States. Travelers won't need to self-quarantine or get tested before or after traveling, unless the destination requires it.

This news is great for anyone who's been looking forward to planning their next big roadtrip. The gas price calculator below can help price-savvy roadtrippers map their routes to find the best deals and allocate their budget for gas.

So, with that, where should you be stopping for the best deal for your tank?
